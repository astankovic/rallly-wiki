This section covers participating in polls and casting votes, designed for participants, guests, or anyone responding to a poll shared by an organizer. It guides you through selecting availability on dates or options using **Yes**, **No**, or **If need be** votes, submitting responses anonymously or with details, adding comments, and viewing live results as they update. This fits into the participant experience after polls are created and shared—see [Creating and Sharing Polls](creating-and-sharing-polls.md) for organizers and [Managing Polls](managing-polls.md) for editing or finalizing polls. Related topics include team collaboration in [Spaces and Team Collaboration](spaces-and-team-collaboration.md) and notifications in [User Settings and Preferences](user-settings-and-preferences.md).

## Overview
As a participant, you access a shared poll link via email, chat, or directly in your browser—no account required for basic voting. The poll displays a list of date or option slots with toggle buttons for quick voting. Live tallies update in real-time for everyone, showing counts or charts of **Yes**, **No**, and **If need be** responses. You can submit anonymously, provide a name for display, or add an email for edit access. After voting, use a dropdown next to your name to revise selections.

## Accessing a Poll
- Open the unique poll link provided by the organizer.
- The poll page shows:
  - Poll title and description at the top.
  - A grid or list of **date slots** or **options** (e.g., times, locations).
  - Under each slot: Toggle buttons labeled **Yes**, **If need be**, **No**.
  - A **Continue** button at the bottom after voting.
  - Live **results section** below votes, with bar charts or counts per option.
  - Optional **comments field** for free-text feedback.

> [!NOTE]  
> Unselected options default to a **No** vote when you submit.

## Vote Options
Use these to indicate your availability for each date or option slot.

| Vote Type    | Meaning                          |
|--------------|----------------------------------|
| **Yes**     | *Fully available and prefer this.* |
| **If need be** | *Available if required, but not ideal.* |
| **No**      | *Unavailable.*                  |

Click the buttons under each slot to cycle through options—your selection highlights immediately.
