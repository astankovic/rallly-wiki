This section covers inviting participants to your poll using a unique shareable URL, along with options like email invites, copying the link, or embedding the poll. It's designed for poll creators who have already set up their poll and options, making it easy to collect responses from others. Inviting participants follows naturally after [Adding Poll Options](adding-poll-options.md) and leads into monitoring votes in [Viewing Results](viewing-results.md) and [Participating and Voting](participating-and-voting.md). For team-based polls, combine this with features in [Spaces and Team Collaboration](spaces-and-team-collaboration.md).

## Overview
The inviting process centers on generating and distributing a unique **Invite Link** for your poll, accessible via a dedicated page (e.g., `/invite/[unique-id]`). This link allows anyone to view the poll, submit availability, and add comments without needing an account. You can share it directly, send email invites to specific people, copy it for messaging apps, or embed it on websites. As responses come in, track participation through participant counts, views, and live results updates. This keeps everything simple and focused on gathering preferences quickly.

## Accessing the Share Options
From your poll's main page:
- Locate the **Share** button, typically in the top-right toolbar or actions menu.
- Click **Share** to open the **Share Dialog**, which displays your unique **Invite Link** prominently.
- The dialog may also show preview thumbnails or social media previews (like Open Graph images) based on your poll's title and author.

> [!NOTE]  
> The **Invite Link** is unique to each poll and remains active until you finalize or delete the poll. It's public by default but respects any poll visibility settings.
